FocusBit does not store or process any personal data. Desktop application does not collect telemetry. All payments on this website are processed by [Paddle](https://paddle.com/) and are regulated by [Paddle privacy policy](https://paddle.com/privacy/) and [Paddle terms of use](https://paddle.com/legal/).

FocuBit is an offline-first software. We do not offer cloud syncronization or online accounts. All data stays on your local machine.
